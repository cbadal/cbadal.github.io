I am a strategist interested in the ways institutional forms are reshaped through design, technology, and belief.

Previously, I was Lead Strategist at Consortia, a research, strategy, and design office in New York City, and a consultant at Are.na, a social platform for collaborative research. I was selected as an inaugural member of GSAPP Incubator at NEW INC, the New Museum's shared workspace for art, design, and technology. I was also a fellow at IdeasCity Arles, a residency program exploring rural-urban revitalization with arts and culture as a driving force. My writing on the evolving landscape of museums in China has been published in LEAP magazine. I studied architecture at Columbia University (BA '14).

Clients include: Audi, Harvard GSD, Microsoft, Graham Foundation, Chicago Architecture Biennial, Adidas, The Annenberg Foundation, Louis Vuitton
