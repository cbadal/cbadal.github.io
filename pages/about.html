Christina Badal is Director at Consortia, a research, strategy, and design office in New York City. She is currently based out of GSAPP Incubator at NEW INC, the New Museum's shared workspace for art, design, and technology. She is helping advise at Are.na, a platform for collaborative research, and recently participated as a Fellow at IdeasCity Arles. She graduated from Columbia University in 2014, where she studied architecture.
